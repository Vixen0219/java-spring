About CIPR

The 7th International Conference on Computational Intelligence in Pattern Recognition (CIPR) will provide a common platform for the presentation of original research findings, exchange of ideas, and dissemination of innovative, practical development experiences in different aspects and fields of data analysis and pattern recognition. After five successful versions (CIPR-2019, CIPR-2020, CIPR 2021, CIPR 2022, CIPR-2023, and CIPR-2024), CIPR-2025 will enlighten the advances of 20th Century Computational Intelligence Techniques in the field of Pattern Recognition. The conference aims at bringing together researchers, scientists, engineers, industrial professionals, and scholar students in their domain of interest. Also, it concentrates on both theory and practices from around the world in all the areas of related disciplines of Pattern Recognition. Other than the main allied domain of pattern recognition, this conference aims to provide rapid dissemination of important results in soft computing, data mining problems, rough set and fuzzy set theory, evolutionary computations, neural science and neural network systems, image processing, combinatorial pattern matching, social network analysis, audio and video data analysis, data mining in a dynamic environment, bioinformatics, hybrid computing, big data analytics, internet of things, deep learning etc. It promotes novel and high-quality research findings, and innovative solutions to challenging Engineering problems; the conference seeks to continuously advance the state-of-the-art in recent developments as well as the other promising areas of advanced computing. Original and unpublished research papers from all disciplines including theory, practice, experimentation, and review work highlighting specific research domains for presentation in the technical sessions of the conference will be invited from researchers, academicians, and professionals throughout the globe.
