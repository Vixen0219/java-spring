Submission Details

The Original unpublished Research Papers, Articles & Working papers on the topics related to the theme are invited for presentation/publication in the conference proceedings. Full papers in strict Springer LNNS format within 10-15 pages must be submitted online through the link Microsoft CMT. The conference proceedings will be published in Springer LNNS Series. Some of the selected high quality papers presented in CIPR-2025 with extended versions will be published in some reputed International Peer Reviewed SCI & SCOPUS indexed Journals (In process).
